What is Counter-Strike: Global Offensive?


Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS: GO) is a first-person shooter video game developed by Valve Corporation in cooperation with Hidden Path Entertainment, and is the fourth game in the Counter-Strike saga, not counting Counter-Strike: Online. It was released to the market on August 21, 2012 for Microsoft Windows, Mac OS X and Xbox 360 platforms, while it was delayed on PlayStation 3. The Linux version was later available to the public on September 22, 2014.Global Offensive had a beta accessible only by invitation on Steam, at the time PC Gamer magazine announced that the video game would be officially launched on August 21, 2012 at a price of 10.99, although it is now free. Thanks to this, its player base increased from 20 to 45 million, making it retake its place within the most played games in recent years.

According to the developers, the game has the same mechanics as its predecessors, although with some new ones. game modes, and does not allow the evolution of the characters thanks to hours of gameplay (as happens in games Call of Duty or Battlefield), you only need to be able. It is available for Windows, Mac, Linux, SteamOS, Xbox360 and PlayStation 3, Xbox One. The version for PlayStation 3 supports DualShock 3, PlayStation Move or keyboard and USB mouse.At the presentation of the game in China in 2017 it was announced that the game would have Valve's new graphics engine, Source 2. Which for now is only implemented in Panorama (the current graphical interface of the game). On August 26, 2011, a trailer and a video were shown showing some of the news. The closed beta was released for testing on November 30, 2011, with two maps available Dust and Dust2 along with eight weapon models.3 This closed beta was only available to 10,000 people who obtained a key at video game conventions where Valve distributed them.



Matchmaking

In this new installment of Counter Strike, a lot of interest has been given to the competitive mode, including an online game search engine, where a 5 vs 5 game is played at the best of 30 rounds (15 on each side), that is, the one that manage to win 16 rounds wins the game. At first, only European and American servers were available, but with the boom in South America they have added servers in Brazil so that there are no problems with pinghigh. The way to match the opponents is through a rank, which is obtained by winning 10 games, in this way the system evaluates the performance in each one and generates a rank that once obtained can be raised or lowered (always on the basis of your skills and the number of games lost or won). You can do the search for a solo game or from 2 to 4 more people, making a total of 5 in the last case, leaving the possibility that "a premade" has total freedom to play on the same team without problems.
